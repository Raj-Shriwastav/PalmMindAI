========================================================================
PALMMIND AI - COMPANY CULTURE & WORK PERKS INFORMATION SHEET
========================================================================

Welcome to PalmMind AI! We are a privacy-first, edge-computing oriented AI Research
and Engineering company specializing in fully local agentic setups, CUDA-accelerated
microservices, and highly optimized RAG pipelines.

CORE CULTURE PILLARS:
1. Async-First Communication: We coordinate across global time zones primarily
   through written specs, git commits, and Slack threads. Meetings are kept to a minimum.
2. Privacy-Centric Philosophy: We believe private data belongs to the user. We never
   forward customer database schemas, proprietary documents, or server credentials
   to third-party public LLM cloud endpoints.
3. Local Execution: Our engineers run local test environments using Docker Compose,
   llama.cpp, Qdrant, and Redis to mirror the production topology.

EMPLOYEE BENEFITS & PERKS:
- Flexible Work Hours: Pick your own working hours. Core overlap time is strictly between
  13:00 and 16:00 UTC.
- Hardware Stipend: Every engineer receives a $3,000 yearly stipend to upgrade their GPUs,
  host machines, memory devices, or local test rigs.
- Learning Budget: $1,500 annual allowance for textbooks, LLM API keys, research paper
  subscriptions, and attending global deep learning conferences (e.g. NeurIPS, ICML).
- Unlimited Paid Time Off (PTO): A minimum of 20 days off per year is mandatory to prevent burnout.
